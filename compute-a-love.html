COMPUTE-A-LOVE

Hand-activated dioramic "computer" possibly predicting your luck in love.

Exhibited:

  • GLI.TC/H 2011 Festival, PLANETART – Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • GLI.TC/H 2011 Festival, MB Labs – Chicago, IL

Coin-operated computer for detecting your lack of luck in love. "Put your hand on me now. The future awaits you."

A dioramic depiction of despair.

An unlucky in love gallery goer at the GLI.TC/H Festival.

Salvaging a coin-operated "Compu-Pulse" chassis.

I thrifted this really depressing looking bathroom set which got me to thinking about little scenes of bleak bathrooms and despair. The Compu-Pulse chassis was a perfect size for a small little diorama that is revealed when inserting your finger.

Bathroom detail photo with hair dryer in partially filled bathtub.
