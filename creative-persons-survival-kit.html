Creative Person's Survival Kit

I made my creative person's "bug out bag" for on-the-go ideation and making. Also, I was adopted by a puppy!

I tried this experiment for the Opposable Thumbs episode 50 podcast, a creative challenge show I co-hosted with Taylor Hokanson. On each episode we invite a guest to take a 2 week creative challenge with us and we talk about our creative journey from ideation to completion. This episode's challenge was "Survival."

I often use found materials and I like to make art in strange places. These are the tools I often bring with me. Annnnd theeeeen a puppy walked into my life. What do you take with you to stay creative?
