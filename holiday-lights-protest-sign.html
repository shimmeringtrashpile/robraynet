Listen to Tiny Insect Footsteps

Using a record needle, aluminum foil, a phono preamp, and a bit of sugar I was able to turn a record needle into an extremely sensitive recording device. Give it a try!

I tried this experiment for the Opposable Thumbs episode 65 podcast, a creative challenge show I co-hosted with Taylor Hokanson. On each episode we invite a guest to take a 2 week creative challenge with us and we talk about our creative journey from ideation to completion.

This video shows how I used a record needle, a piece of aluminum foil, and a LOT of noise reduction to hear the footsteps of a fly.
