Kissy Face 4x4

I took a user-centered design approach to determine what a 2 year-old wants in a four wheel drive vehicle. They liked their truck green, with cats, and good at kissing people. Truck manufacturers, where you at?

I tried this experiment for the Opposable Thumbs episode 37 podcast, a creative challenge show I co-hosted with Taylor Hokanson. On each episode we invite a guest to take a 2 week creative challenge with us and we talk about our creative journey from ideation to completion. This episode's challenge was "Not My Style."
