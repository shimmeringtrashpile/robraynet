Ping Pong Throwies

Cut a slit into some ping-pong balls. Slip in a magnet. Spray paint a cool color. Adorn your town with magnetic throwable ping-pong balls!

I tried this experiment for the Opposable Thumbs episode 56 podcast, a creative challenge show I co-hosted with Taylor Hokanson. On each episode we invite a guest to take a 2 week creative challenge with us and we talk about our creative journey from ideation to completion. This episode's challenge was "Anonymous."
