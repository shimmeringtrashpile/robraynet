Bucky's Animal Spirit

intervention game video public electronics

I install this arcade game disguised as an ATM in a downtown/public areas. People insert their card expecting to withdraw money. Instead they play a game helping Bucky and Toothy Beaver get to the public library!

A few folks were simply confused and walked away, but most people help Bucky and Toothy rekindle their animal spirits!

Check out the "Building Bucky" Flickr photoset if you'd like to see how I constructed the ATM cabinet.

Installed surreptitiously in:

  • Troy, New York
  • Chicago, Illinois
  • San Francisco, California
  • Los Angeles, California
  • Austin, Texas

The ATM-style keypad interface is used to help get Bucky the Beaver to the public library. He can jump and shoot his slingshot at menacing satelites trying to overwhelm him with subscription services.
