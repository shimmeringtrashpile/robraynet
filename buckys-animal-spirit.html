Bucky's Animal Spirit

intervention game video public electronics

I install this arcade game disguised as an ATM in a downtown/public areas. People insert their ATM card into the machine expecting to withdraw money. Instead they are presented with my intro video featuring Bucky and Toothy Beaver lamenting their financial troubles sharing their doubts about the current economy.

A few folks were simply confused and walked away, but most people help Bucky and Toothy rekindle their animal spirits!

Check out the "Building Bucky" Flickr photoset if you'd like to see how I constructed the ATM cabinet.

Installed surreptitiously in:

  • Troy, New York
  • Chicago, Illinois
  • San Francisco, California
  • Los Angeles, California
  • Austin, Texas
