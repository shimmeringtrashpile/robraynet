Tower and Control Room

Tower and Control Room is an interactive installation for listening to the U.S. R-2508 airspace. This project was created for the Center for Land Use Interpretation's (CLUI) Remote Sensing exhibition.

The 44 foot (13.41 meters) radio tower has two antennae that recieve signals from the R-2508 airspace.
